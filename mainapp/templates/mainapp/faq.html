Frequently Asked Questions

What are NAFakes products?

NAFakes products are imaginary items designed to spark creativity and joy in children through make-believe play.

Simply imagine and play! Our products exist in the realm of imagination, encouraging creative thinking and storytelling.

Are NAFakes products safe?

Absolutely! Since our products are imaginary, they pose no physical risks. However, we recommend adult supervision during playtime.
